My final project is entitled "A Tool For Calculating The Physical Parameters Of Cow Without Physical Contact". This tool is used to measure the weight of cattle without physical contact and without using conventional scales. My final project is entitled "a device for calculating the physical parameters of cattle without touching the body". This tool is used to measure the weight of cattle without physical contact and without using conventional scales. This tool is in the form of software or smartphone application, the way to use it is to take pictures on the C-Mon application from the side of the cow with a distance of about 2-3 meters. This final project was done by 2 people in a team, where I worked on an application in Android Studio software and my friend designed the front end with Figma software.
